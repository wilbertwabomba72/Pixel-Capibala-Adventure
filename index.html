📖 Game Overview

Pixel Capibala Adventure is a thrilling platformer game where you guide an adorable capybara through challenging levels filled with obstacles, collectibles, and exciting adventures. With pixel art graphics and intuitive controls, this game offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages.

🎮 How to Play

Controls:
• Tap left/right side of screen to move
• Tap jump button to jump
• Collect coins and avoid obstacles
• Reach the finish line to complete each level

Tips:
• Watch out for spikes and hazards
• Use springs to reach higher platforms
• Collect all stars for perfect completion
• Unlock new skins in the shop
